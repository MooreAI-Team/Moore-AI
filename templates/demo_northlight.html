About

Started in a kitchen in north Fargo.

It started in 2019, pouring candles on the kitchen table to give away as gifts. One farmers market table led to two, then a website. It has stayed small on purpose.

Everything is made in batches of under 50. We use soy wax because it burns cleaner and longer than paraffin. Cotton wicks because they hold their shape and don't curl.

We sell at the Red River Market on Saturdays and ship anywhere in the US. Orders go out within three days. Free shipping on orders over $40.
