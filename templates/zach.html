Moore AI

Zachary Moore

CEO & Founder

github.com/Zach-A-Moore linkedin.com/in/zach-moore-ai

Zach did not come from a place where starting a company was the expected outcome. In high school, he went through a period of being temporarily homeless. He showed up to college at MSUM without a safety net, without a clear path, and without much more than the decision that things were going to be different.

What happened next says a lot about who he is. Within his first year, he had made the Dean's List both semesters, joined the student tech committee, become president of ACM, and landed an engineering role at Bushel, an agricultural technology company, while still a freshman. Most people spend their first year figuring out where the library is.

He did not stop there. He became a mentor in the STEM learning community, joined DECA, conducted research, and presented that research at NCUR 2026, a national undergraduate research conference. He also found something more important than any line on a resume: a community in Fargo that became a second family.

Moore AI came out of all of that. Not from a business plan or a pitch deck, but from a genuine belief that the right people, working on the right problems, can build something that actually matters. Zach started the company because he had seen firsthand what it looks like when technology works for people and what it costs when it does not.

He leads every client engagement the same way he approached college: with full attention, no excuses, and a stubborn commitment to getting it right.
