Welcome to Summit Roofing Co.

For more than 15 years, Summit Roofing has served homeowners and property managers across the Twin Cities metro. We replace roofs, handle storm damage claims, install gutters, and take on commercial jobs.

Minnesota winters are hard on roofs. Every product we use is chosen for this climate, and every install is backed by a 2-year labor warranty on top of the manufacturer coverage.
