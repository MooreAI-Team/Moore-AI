What We Do

Four types of work.
One crew that does all of it.

No subcontractors. Every job is run by a Summit employee from the estimate through cleanup.

Roof Replacement

Full tear-off down to the deck, inspection, and install. We handle permits, dumpster, and cleanup. Most residential jobs finish in a single day.

Storm Damage & Insurance

Hail, wind, ice damage. We document what we find, walk you through the insurance process, and replace what needs replacing. We deal with adjusters regularly and know what to look for.

Gutters

Seamless aluminum gutters cut and installed on-site to match your roofline. Gutter guard available. We also repair and clean existing gutters.

Commercial Roofing

Low-slope and flat roofing for commercial and multi-family properties. TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen. We work around your schedule and timeline.

About Summit

Started with two guys and a used truck.

Summit started in 2009. We did residential work in the south metro and grew from there. The company now has a crew of eight and does around 150 jobs a year. That number is intentional.

We don't subcontract. Every job is run by a Summit employee from the estimate through cleanup. That's the only way we can stand behind the work with a real warranty.

Licensed in Minnesota and Wisconsin. $2 million general liability. We carry workers' comp on every employee on every job.
